Rogbone is a creative force and sonic innovator, known for defying conventions through their powerful music. Born to deaf, interracial parents and raised in a rural area, they transform experiences of racism and homophobia into art. Influenced by JPEGMAFIA, Tyler, the Creator, and childhood sounds, Rogbone crafts an eclectic mix of hip-hop, experimental, and electronic music with poignant lyrics addressing romantic life and inequality.
