KEVIN ROADARMEL



kevinroadarmel@gmail.com

Utilizing wavy syntheziers, horns, and hard hitting drums, alternative hip-hop rapper, singer, and producer extroardinare Kevin Roadarmel is an artist to look out for. They have recently hit 50 streams on their song "Raspberry Lemonade" in 7 days! Kevin Roadarmel was born in raised in Indiana, PA in the Greater Pittsburgh Region to deaf Jamaican-American and European-American parents. They first started producing at the age of 15 using digital audio workstation FL Studio. At 18, they started rapping over those beats. Inspired by experimental rapper JPEGMAFIA, his vocal performance has been loud, confident, and emphatic ever sincde. Although their parents were deaf, the music of Bob Marley was played constantly in the home. You can hear this influence in the synth and horn-based instrument choice in Kevin's production.
